## 🤖 BOT PERFORMANCE FEES

### Revenue Share Model

**How it works:**
1. User runs a profitable bot
2. Platform takes X% of net profits
3. No fees on losses
4. Monthly settlement

**Fee Structure:**

| Tier | Performance Fee | Max Monthly Cap |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| Free | 20% | $100 |
| Premium | 15% | Unlimited |
| VIP | 10% | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | 5-8% | Custom |

**Example (Premium user):**
```
Month 1: Bot makes $1,000 profit → Platform takes $150 (15%)
Month 2: Bot loses $200 → No fee
Month 3: Bot makes $500 profit → Platform takes $75
```

**Projected Revenue:**

- Year 1: $500K (10K bots running, avg $50 profit/bot/month)
- Year 2: $5M (50K bots running)
- Year 3: $15M (150K bots running)

**Why users accept this:**
- Aligned incentives (we profit when they profit)
- Industry standard (3Commas, Cryptohopper charge similar)
- Optional (can use without bots)
- Transparent (visible in dashboard)

## 👥 COPY TRADING FEES

### Marketplace Model

**How it works:**
1. Top traders share their strategies
2. Followers copy their trades automatically
3. Platform takes commission from profits

**Fee Structure:**

**Trader (Strategy Provider):**
- Earns 15-30% of follower profits (they set rate)
- Minimum track record: 100 trades, 6 months
- Must maintain min 55% win rate

**Follower:**
- Platform fee: 10% of profits
- Trader performance fee: 15-30% (varies)
- Total cost: 25-40% of profits

**Example:**
```
Follower invests $10,000 copying trader
Month profit: $1,000

Platform fee: $100 (10%)
Trader fee: $200 (20% - trader's rate)
Follower keeps: $700 (70%)
```

**Revenue Projection:**
- Year 1: $200K (1,000 active copy relationships)
- Year 2: $2.5M (10K relationships)
- Year 3: $7.5M (30K relationships)

**Trader Incentives:**
- Earn passive income
- Reputation system
- Leaderboard visibility
- VIP tier upgrade (free)
